FAQs



Here are the frequently asked questions most ARMY ask:

  • Where are you located?

    • We are located at One Cedar Wood Place, Tomas Morato Avenue, Quezon City between Winland Residences and Shell

  • Is there an age restriction?

    • Dine-in service is allowed for minors (regardless of vaccination status) and fully vaccinated adults.

  • Are we allowed to take pictures if we are not going to dine-in or take-out at the cafe?

    • Sorry, only customers who dine-in or take-out food are allowed to enter the 3rd and 4th floor cafe area.

  • How long can we stay if we are only going to take-out?

    • Customers for take-out usually stay 15 to 20 minutes.

  • Do you accept credit or debit cards?


    • We only accept cash and Gcash payments at the store.

  • Do you deliver? Who arranges the booking?


    • We accept Grab Pabili.

  • Do you accept private events?

    • Kindly contact us on Facebook.



    Feel free to message us on our Facebook page if you have any concern with dining in, take out, delivery, and reservations. See you there!
