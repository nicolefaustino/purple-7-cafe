Welcome to Purple 7 Café!

Purple 7 is a BTS-themed cafe in Quezon City that offers high quality coffee, pastries, and more! The cafe originally opened in Congressional Avenue in December 2020, and has relocated to a revamped four-floor ARMY haven in Tomas Morato Avenue in 2022.
